Looking for ISKCON Near Me in Pune? Visit ISKCON Hadapsar

If you've been searching for an "ISKCON near me" or "Hare Krishna temple near me in Pune," your search ends here. ISKCON Hadapsar — officially known as Sri Sri Radha Vrindavanchandra Temple — is one of the most easily accessible ISKCON temples in Pune, located on the Pune-Solapur Road in Hadapsar (PIN: 411028).

Whether you are coming from Magarpatta City, Kharadi, Viman Nagar, Wanowrie, Kondhwa, or central Pune, ISKCON Hadapsar is your nearest Radha Krishna temple. The temple is just minutes from Hadapsar and easily reachable by auto, bus, or cab.

About ISKCON — International Society for Krishna Consciousness

ISKCON (International Society for Krishna Consciousness) was founded in 1966 by His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada in New York City. Today, ISKCON has over 700 temples worldwide, making it one of the largest Vaishnava movements in the world. The ISKCON Pune Hadapsar center upholds this legacy by serving thousands of devotees every month.

The philosophy of ISKCON is rooted in the ancient Vedic scriptures — primarily the Bhagavad Gita and Srimad Bhagavatam. Through Harinam Sankirtan (chanting of the Hare Krishna Maha-mantra), distribution of Prasadam (sanctified food), and regular Aarti programs, ISKCON temples like ours in Hadapsar serve as beacons of spiritual light in every city.

Why Visit ISKCON Hadapsar Temple?

There are many reasons why thousands of devotees and visitors choose ISKCON Hadapsar as their preferred Hare Krishna temple in Pune:

1. Stunning Deity Darshan: The presiding deities, Sri Sri Radha Vrindavanchandra, are beautifully decorated and offer a profoundly moving Darshan experience. The deities are dressed in fresh outfits for each Aarti, creating a uniquely beautiful visual experience every time you visit.

2. Powerful Kirtan & Aarti: The Aarti programs at ISKCON Hadapsar — especially the evening Gaura Aarti at 7:00 PM — are electrifying experiences. The sound of mridanga drums, karatalas, and collective Hare Krishna chanting creates an atmosphere that devotees describe as transcendental.

3. Free Prasadam Distribution: Pure vegetarian Prasadam (food offered to Lord Krishna) is distributed to all visitors. The Sunday Love Feast offers an unlimited Prasadam meal completely free of charge, making ISKCON Hadapsar one of the most welcoming spiritual centers in Pune.

4. Bhagavad Gita Classes: Daily Bhagavad Gita classes, Srimad Bhagavatam discourses, and special festivals offer ongoing spiritual education for all seekers — beginners and advanced devotees alike.

Sunday Love Feast Pune — Every Sunday at ISKCON Hadapsar

The Sunday Love Feast is the most celebrated weekly event at ISKCON Hadapsar, Pune. Held every Sunday evening, it includes:
