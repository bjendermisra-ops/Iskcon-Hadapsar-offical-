About the Temple

ISKCON Hadapsar is a center of spiritual education and culture. We aim to inspire people to lead a God-centered life through the teachings of Bhagavad Gita and Srimad Bhagavatam.

Founder-Acharya

His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada

We are dedicated to serving the mission of Srila Prabhupada by distributing the holy name and transcendental literature.
