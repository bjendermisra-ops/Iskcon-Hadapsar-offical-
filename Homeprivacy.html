Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Feb 5, 2025

Overview & Purpose

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which the ISKCON Hadapsar official website collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected while using our digital devotional services.

Data Tech & Security

Platform Architecture: This website is engineered using Google Firebase scalable infrastructure. We collect minimal operational data (cookies for language/theme settings) to ensure the site runs smoothly on your browser.

Any future user data (for donations or profiles) will be encrypted and stored on secure cloud servers. We do not sell data to advertisers.

Cookies & Integrations

We use local browser storage (Cookies) strictly to save your preferences (Dark Mode, Hindi/Marathi Language). External services like Google Maps (Locations) and YouTube (Live Darshan) may process data under their own policies when used.
